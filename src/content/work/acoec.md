## The Project

ACOEC (Asociación para la Cooperación entre Comunidades) is a Spanish development NGO working in international cooperation. In September 2023, they launched a local awareness project focused on four interconnected themes: social and solidarity economy, ethical finance, ecofeminism and feminist economics, and critical consumption.

The goal was to make existing educational content accessible online — giving the campaign a clear, navigable digital home.

[Visit the website →](https://infoess.org/)

## Scope & Responsibilities

**Web content adaptation:** ACOEC provided the infographics and visual materials already developed for the campaign. My role was to translate that content into a structured web format — adapting the information architecture, copy and visual layout so the material worked as a website rather than a standalone graphic.

**Coordination:** Managed the project through a series of coordination meetings with the organisation, ensuring the final result aligned with their communication objectives and the tone of the campaign.

## What This Project Demonstrates

Not every project is large-scale. This one was deliberately scoped, fast-turnaround and output-focused — and delivering it well required understanding quickly what the organisation needed and adapting existing materials without losing their intent.

It also reflects a practical skill: knowing how content that works in one format (infographic) needs to be restructured to work in another (web), rather than simply transposing it.

## Impact

- Website delivered within a focused, short-turnaround project timeline
- Educational content on 4 thematic areas made accessible in digital format
- Coordination with organisational team from brief to delivery
