## The Project

With Your Own Voice was a 2-year European Union funded project bringing together 6 partner organisations across Spain, Greece, Italy and Portugal — with the shared goal of amplifying underrepresented voices through participatory communication methodologies.

My role covered the full communications lifecycle: strategy, coordination, reporting, training and digital presence.

[Visit the project website →](https://withyourownvoice.org/)

## Scope & Responsibilities

**Partner coordination:** Managed ongoing communication and alignment across 24 partner representatives from 6 organisations in 4 countries — in English and Spanish. Coordinating across time zones, institutional cultures and funding requirements demanded both precision and adaptability.

**KPI reporting & EU compliance:** Designed and maintained the project's KPI tracking framework across all activity areas. Consolidated multi-source data into structured Excel reporting for quarterly submissions to the funding agency — translating complex programme activity into the metrics and narrative format required by EU reporting standards.

**Workshop facilitation:** Designed and delivered training sessions for 100+ participants across partner countries, with a team of 5 facilitators. Sessions focused on participatory communication, voice and self-representation.

**Web development & content:** Built and maintained the project website in WordPress — responsible for information architecture, content strategy and multilingual content management throughout the 2-year programme.

**Narrative & documentation:** Wrote all justification reports, activity summaries and stakeholder communications — ensuring that the project's impact was communicated clearly to both funding bodies and public audiences.

## What This Project Demonstrates

With Your Own Voice is evidence of what communications strategy looks like at scale — not campaigns, but sustained coordination across institutions, languages and reporting requirements over two years.

It also illustrates a core part of my professional approach: **data without narrative doesn't drive decisions, and narrative without data doesn't hold up to scrutiny.** Every report submitted to the funding agency had to balance both — and so did every workshop, every stakeholder update, and every page of the website.

## Impact

- 6 partner organisations coordinated across Spain, Greece, Italy and Portugal
- 24 partner representatives managed throughout the programme lifecycle
- 100+ workshop participants across partner countries
- 5 facilitators coordinated
- 2 years of EU compliance reporting delivered on time
